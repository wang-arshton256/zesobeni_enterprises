About Zesobeni Enterprises Bbotera

We are a family owned enterprise on about 15 acres of fragmated land. The name is derived from a combitation of Zesobeni my village in my ancestral district Sirinko and my adopted home of Bbotera in Kyotera District. The name represents the combination of both cultures and brings out the best from both.

Our mission is to provide our customers with quality produce and products through traditional and modern farming practices at elatively affordable prices.

Our goal is to be fully operational by 2023, To have a clientel that is local, regional and national by 2023. To recruit the less fortunate and train them for seasonal jobs on the farm, cottage industry and reatail trade. To be self sufficient in almost everything we need in production and processing sections.
